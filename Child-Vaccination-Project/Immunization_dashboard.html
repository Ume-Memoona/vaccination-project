Vaccination coverage for Pakistan

These data show the latest WHO/UNICEF Estimates of National Immunization Coverage (WUENIC) for Pakistan. They are based on data reported until 7 July 2022. The current release of the WUENIC with data up to 2021 (released in 2022) includes estimates for WHO Member States, including those that did not report 2021 data. For these non-reporting countries, estimates were extrapolated from the last point informed by empirical data. Non-reporting countries represent ≈6% of the global 2021 cohort. WUENIC data time series are updated annually in July based on available reported data and survey results. Coverage data can help to identify gaps, and can be viewed globally, regionally, or at the country level by selecting an antigen to view the coverage data time series.

Pakistan vaccination coverage over-time

These data show trends in vaccination coverage for Pakistan by selected antigens over time. The source of the data displayed here is the WHO/UNICEF Estimates of National Immunization Coverage (WUENIC) which are updated annually in July based on available reported data and survey results. The current release of the WUENIC with data up to 2021 (released in 2022) includes estimates for WHO Member States, including those that did not report 2021 data. For these non-reporting countries, estimates were extrapolated from the last point informed by empirical data. Non-reporting countries represent ≈6% of the global 2021 cohort. Please be sure to reference the 2021 revision, data as at July 2022 as the data is used.
